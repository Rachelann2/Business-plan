Business Plan

Fitness studio that offers bootcamp classes and cycle class 7 days a week. Weekdays will have a specific body party that each bootcamp class would be targeting, for ex:
Monday: Lower Body Day Day
Tuesday: Upper Body Day
Wednesday: Conditioning Day
Thursday: Core & Stability Day
Friday: Full Body Day
While the cycle classes will be a rhythm based class, based on the music and the energy of each instructor.
However, each client will be offered the chance to rent a (free with a purchased class or membership) heart rate monitor that goes around one's lower chest area to get an accurate measurement of one's heart rate.
SPECIFIC TO OUR BRAND
Our APP will allow each customer to enter in their weight and height when creating an account to allow the heart rate monitor to accurately measure calories burned based off of each clients information (Age, Gender, Weight, and height) Our fitness studio will allow each customer to get the weight lifting and cardio to get the perfect combination for a healthier lifestyle. As well as the option of calories burned for dietary needs

Website and Mobile App

My goal as a certified full stack web developer is to create a business with an emphasis on the mobile app.
In today’s modern day, technology is everything.
My goal is to create a fitness app that revolves around
Class sign up and purchasing
A profile given to each client that allows them to enter in their age, gender, weight and height as well as their preference on gaining muscle, losing weight or maintaining weight
Then based off of the calories burned in each class they take, (specific to Hustle House) the app will offer the calories burned + calories in a day each profiled client can eat in order to gain muscle, lose weight, or maintain weight
Zingfit (used by Barry’s Bootcamp) is a website that is used to create a class list for instructor pay, client count per class, and payroll of each employee

MVP (base model app):
· Sign up/Sign in (buy classes or memberships)
· Browse and book classes, view details (via Integration with Zingfit)
· Browse class/instructor bio’s
· Heart rate, calories burned and intensity monitor (via integration with heart rate monitor)
· Browse classes history, view class ratings

Advanced (plus model):
· All the features of MVP plus the following:
· Set and track a goal
· Food and activities diary (add and view entries)
· Daily plan and calculations based on diary entries
· Weekly/monthly reports
· Meals plan/recommendations based on diary entries

Web Admin
· View/manage schedule and classes
· Browse members, view and edit member details
· Browse payrolls (via Integration with Zingfit)

Why I Want This

My name is Rachel Silverthorn. I want to create a business called Hustle House that not only will create a new community, but also a program to better the lives of everyone who is involved.
According to the National Institutes of Health, obesity and overweight together are the second leading cause of preventable death in the United States, close behind tobacco use. An estimated 300,000 deaths per year are due to the obesity epidemic, Hustle House will create a program to help ordinary people learn how to eat and exercise to maintain a healthy weight and I want to create the technology in order to program such a widespread solution.
From a young age I have always struggled with body image issues, being a young girl in today's society there are so many expectations on looks with unrealistic images of famous models. I grew up with parents that always chose to build me up, my step mom would take me to many different fitness studios, trying crossfit, pilates, yoga, spin; you name it, I have tried it, working on ways to feel the most confident in my image. My dad has always made me feel confident not only in my own skin but also throughout school, when I struggled academically he was always the first person to sit down and help me understand.
I soon went off to University of California, Davis, following in my older brothers footsteps (he also went to davis) I soon began to struggle with how hard the classes were. With UC Davis being one of the top 5 universities in the country, the stakes of success came with great pressure. What most people do not see is how hard it is to work through these classes at the pace of a quarter system but the information of a semester. Not surprisingly, depression was increased throughout students in the college campus, “At UC Davis, 29 enrolled students have committed suicide over the past 20 years, with never more than three students killing themselves in a single year, Rodolfa said. Additionally, the center has hospitalized about 12 to 15 suicidal students a year.”- Amy Agronis.Providing that depression is a serious issue that is partnered with the demands of a top university.
I noticed that I began to struggle with anxiety, and spin was my release of the demands. My brother has also fought with anxiety and depression. We didn’t have parents that expected us to get straight A’s and reprimand us if I wasn’t on the Dean's list, but we could only imagine what the students felt like whose parents were more demanding of them. All of these issues are brought onto students at such a young age, many do not know how to deal with the struggle of not being good enough.
That is where our family comes in; I found my niche studying exercise biology at UC Davis, learning the health benefits behind exercise and eating healthy; and how they change the neurological releases within the body. In shorthand, exercise is the leading “medicine” or antidote for depression, diabetes, some cancers and the ability to live longer. Exercise is something so simple as walking your dog around the block, or healthy eating as cutting out foods that most people don’t even realize is bad for them. Did you know that a mango has more sugar in it than a Coca Cola? While most people will reach for the mango instead of coke, what do people really know about food and exercise other than whats the new fad diet or class to take.
Hustle House is a program that I want to build based on getting to know each and every customer on a deeper basis than any other workout program. The app/website program will start by asking about weight loss, gain or maintain. Then figure out if the client struggles with any type of mental illness, whether it be depression or anxiety or something more; I want each client to feel like they are getting a personalized coach to better not only their outside image but also there inside self-esteem.
As a young UC Davis college grad, I not only have the time and dedication, I have the heart and experience behind it all. From being a young girl struggling with my inner self-esteem to grow into this role of helping others create a better self-image there is nothing I am more passionate about. Leading this community with a heavy heart to create a stronger commitment to living without the social demands of perfection. Now is a time to be vulnerable and work to create a better life for not only oneself but for everyone else around them, inspiring each next generation to work on themselves inside out rather than outside in.
I have seen first hand what body-image, depression, anxiety and hurt self-esteem does to a person and I believe I have the power to help create a better system through my program of exercise and healthy lifestyles to build our community up. While my family has helped me through each determining step in life, I want the same gift brought to our community. My dad is willing to put his faith into my brother and I to create this program in the Sacramento community. My step mom has dedicated her time and hard work to help us accomplish this goal. As a family we want to accomplish this goal to building a community of people who want to better themselves, when they might not even know they need it.
Everyone has a struggle whether it be there own or someone close to them, I want to give this community a chance to change, to work to create a better self-image. It starts now, with Hustle House.
